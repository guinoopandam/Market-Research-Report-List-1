<p><p>The IoT smart proximity sensors market consists of various types catering to different applications. Capacitive proximity sensors detect changes in capacitance caused by nearby objects, making them ideal for non-metallic materials. Inductive proximity sensors identify metallic objects through changes in inductance, commonly used in industrial settings. Photoelectric proximity sensors utilize light beams to sense the presence of objects, suitable for diverse environments. Other types may include ultrasonic or microwave sensors, expanding the range of applications in IoT systems.</p></p>
