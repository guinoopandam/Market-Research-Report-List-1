Filter coatings selectively transmit specific wavelengths. High reflective coatings maximize reflectivity for mirrors. Transparent conductive coatings provide electrical conductivity while remaining clear. Partial reflective coatings allow some light to pass while reflecting others. Other coatings encompass specialized applications tailored to unique requirements.</p></p>
