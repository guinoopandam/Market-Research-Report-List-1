<p><p>The paper packaging tape market comprises two main types: adhesive and non-adhesive. Adhesive paper packaging tape features a sticky backing, allowing it to bond securely to various surfaces, making it ideal for sealing packages and ensuring secure shipments. Non-adhesive paper packaging tape, on the other hand, relies on mechanical fastening methods, such as tying or wrapping, without an adhesive surface. Both types are environmentally friendly alternatives to plastic tapes, catering to sustainable packaging needs across various industries.</p></p>
