<p><p>The insomnia treatment drugs market is primarily divided into two categories: prescription sleep aids and over-the-counter (OTC) sleep aids. Prescription sleep aids, such as benzodiazepines and non-benzodiazepine medications, are typically recommended by healthcare professionals for more severe insomnia cases and require a doctor's approval. In contrast, OTC sleep aids, which often include antihistamines and herbal supplements, are available without a prescription and provide a milder approach for occasional sleeplessness. Both types address sleep disturbances but differ in availability and regulation.</p></p>
