<p><strong>The Endogenous Creatinine Measurement Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jaffe's Kinetic Method</li><li>Enzymatic Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endogenous Creatinine Measurement Kits Market consists of two primary types: Jaffe's Kinetic Method and Enzymatic Method. The Jaffe's Kinetic Method relies on the reaction of creatinine with alkaline picrate, producing a color change that is measured spectrophotometrically. It is widely used due to its simplicity and cost-effectiveness. The Enzymatic Method, on the other hand, uses specific enzymes to quantify creatinine levels, offering higher specificity and accuracy. Both methods cater to various clinical and laboratory needs, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
