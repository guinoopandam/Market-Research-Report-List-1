<p><p>IT Infrastructure Management Software is categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed directly on a company's hardware and is managed internally, offering more control and customization. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted on external servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and maintenance by third-party providers. Organizations choose between these types based on factors like infrastructure needs, budget, security requirements, and the desire for flexibility in managing IT resources.</p></p>
