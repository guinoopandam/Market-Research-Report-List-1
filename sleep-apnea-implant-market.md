<p><p>The sleep apnea implant market is primarily categorized into neuromodulator implants and tongue obstruction implants. Neuromodulator implants focus on stimulating nerves to enhance airway muscle function, reducing apnea episodes. In contrast, tongue obstruction implants are designed to position the tongue to prevent airway blockage during sleep. Both types aim to provide effective, surgical solutions for obstructive sleep apnea, catering to patients who seek alternatives to traditional CPAP therapy or lifestyle changes, ultimately improving sleep quality and overall health.</p></p>
