<p><ul><li>Active Q Switch</li><li>Passive Q Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Q-switches are crucial components in laser technology, particularly for generating high-intensity, pulsed laser beams. Active Q-switches utilize external energy sources to control the laser's output, enabling precise timing for pulse generation. In contrast, passive Q-switches rely on saturable absorbers that automatically modulate the laser's output without external intervention. Both types are widely used across various industrial applications, including materials processing, marking, and medical procedures, facilitating advancements in efficiency and precision in laser operations.</p></p>
