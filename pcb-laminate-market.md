<p><p>The PCB laminate market consists of various types, each catering to different applications and performance requirements. Glass fabric laminates are widely used for their mechanical strength and thermal stability. Epoxy resin laminates offer excellent electrical properties and are commonly utilized in general electronics. Kraft paper laminates provide cost-effective solutions for lower-tier applications. Phenolic resin laminates are valued for their high heat resistance and are typically found in high-frequency applications, making each type essential for specific market needs.</p></p>
