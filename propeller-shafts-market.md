<p><ul><li>Single Piece Shaft</li><li>Multi Piece Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The propeller shafts market is divided into two main types: single-piece shafts and multi-piece shafts. Single-piece shafts are manufactured as a single, continuous unit, providing enhanced strength and reduced weight, making them ideal for applications with limited space. In contrast, multi-piece shafts consist of two or more segments connected by universal joints, allowing for greater flexibility and easier installation in larger vehicles. Each type caters to distinct vehicle requirements, optimizing performance and efficiency in power transmission.</p></p>
