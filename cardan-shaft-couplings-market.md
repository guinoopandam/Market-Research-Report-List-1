Solid hub couplings offer robust performance with a single-piece design, ideal for high-torque applications. Split hub couplings provide ease of installation and maintenance due to their divided design, accommodating misalignments effectively. Tapered couplings feature a conical shape that allows for secure and easy assembly, often used in applications requiring precise alignment and quick disassembly.</p></p>
