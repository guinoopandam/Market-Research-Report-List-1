<p><p>The PTFE-lined butterfly valves market features three main types: wafer type, double-flanged type, and lug type. Wafer type valves are compact and designed for installation between two flanges, offering ease of use in tight spaces. Double-flanged valves provide robust sealing and are suitable for high-pressure applications with flanges on both sides. Lug-type valves feature threaded lugs for mounting, allowing for easy removal and maintenance without disturbing the pipeline. Each type caters to specific installation needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
