<p><strong>The Cardiopulmonary Bypass Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Roller Pump</li><li>Double Roller Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiopulmonary bypass pump market is primarily categorized into single roller and double roller pumps. Single roller pumps use one roller to propel blood through tubing, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, double roller pumps employ two rollers, providing more consistent blood flow and reducing hemolysis, which is the breakdown of blood cells. Both types are crucial in cardiac surgeries, with varying applications depending on the required flow rates and patient needs, influencing market demand and technological advancements.</p></p>
