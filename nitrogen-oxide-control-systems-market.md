<p><p>The Nitrogen Oxide (NOx) control systems market includes two primary technologies: Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR) and Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR). SNCR utilizes injected reagents at high temperatures to facilitate a chemical reaction that reduces NOx emissions without catalysts. In contrast, SCR employs a catalyst to enhance the reduction process, allowing for more effective NOx removal at lower temperatures. Both systems aim to meet stringent regulatory requirements while improving air quality and reducing environmental impact in various industrial applications.</p></p>
