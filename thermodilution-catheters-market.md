<p><p>Thermodilution catheters are specialized medical devices used primarily in measuring cardiac output and hemodynamic monitoring. These catheters operate on the principle of thermodilution, where a cold indicator solution is injected into the bloodstream, and the resulting temperature change is measured to calculate cardiac output. They are commonly utilized in critical care settings, including intensive care units and during various cardiovascular surgeries, providing crucial information for patient management.</p><p>The Thermodilution Catheters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.9% during the forecast period, driven by factors such as an increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in catheter technology, and a growing emphasis on better patient monitoring systems.
