<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The 3D Solid-state LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Based Scanning</li><li>Phase Array</li><li>Non-Scanning Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Solid-state LiDAR market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **MEMS Based Scanning**: Utilizes Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems to scan laser beams across a scene, offering high precision with compact design.</p><p>2. **Phase Array**: Employs multiple laser beams energized simultaneously, allowing rapid data acquisition without mechanical movement, benefiting from faster scanning and robustness.</p><p>3. **Non-Scanning Flash**: Emits a broad laser pulse covering an area all at once, capturing comprehensive depth information instantly, making it ideal for rapid imaging and obstacle detection.</p></p>
