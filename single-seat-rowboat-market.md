<p><p>The single seat rowboat market is primarily divided into two types: sculling and sweep rowboats. Sculling rowboats feature two oars and allow the rower to propel the boat using both hands, providing greater maneuverability and speed. In contrast, sweep rowboats utilize a single, longer oar, typically held by one rower on one side, offering enhanced stability for teamwork and coordination in larger crews. Both types cater to different rower preferences and competitive demands, influencing design and functionality.</p></p>
