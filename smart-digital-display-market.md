<p><p>The Smart Digital Display market comprises various technologies, primarily LCD and LED displays. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) uses liquid crystals and a backlight to produce images, offering excellent color and clarity, making it suitable for indoor use. In contrast, LED (Light Emitting Diode) displays use semiconductor technology to emit light, providing brighter images with lower energy consumption. LED displays are favored for outdoor applications due to their superior visibility and durability, driving growth across advertising, retail, and entertainment sectors.</p></p>
