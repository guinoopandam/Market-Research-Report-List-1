<p><p>The immunization syringe market comprises three main types: conventional syringes, safety syringes, and prefilled syringes. Conventional syringes are widely used for a variety of injections but pose risks of needle-stick injuries. Safety syringes are designed with safety mechanisms to minimize these risks, enhancing protection for healthcare workers. Prefilled syringes contain a pre-measured dose of vaccine, ensuring accuracy and simplicity in administration, while also reducing preparation time and medication waste, making them increasingly popular in healthcare settings.</p></p>
