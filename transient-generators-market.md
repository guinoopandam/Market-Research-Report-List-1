<p><strong>The Transient Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surge Generators</li><li>Burst Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transient generators are specialized devices used to simulate short-duration electrical disturbances in power systems. The surge generator produces high-voltage transients, typically seen in lightning strikes or switching events, aiding in the testing of equipment for surge withstand capabilities. Burst generators, on the other hand, create shorter, high-energy pulses, often employed to assess circuit performance during brief, intense disruptions, such as faults. Together, these generators are crucial for ensuring the reliability and resilience of electrical and electronic systems under transient conditions.</p></p>
