<p><strong>The Magnesium Alloy Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast</li><li>Forged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnesium alloy wheels are categorized primarily into two types: cast and forged. Cast wheels are made by pouring molten magnesium into molds, resulting in a cost-effective solution with good performance for everyday use. In contrast, forged wheels are produced by shaping solid magnesium under high pressure, offering superior strength, lightweight properties, and enhanced performance, making them popular in high-performance and racing applications. Both types are valued for their ability to reduce weight, improve fuel efficiency, and enhance vehicle handling.</p></p>
