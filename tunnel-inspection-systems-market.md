<p><p>The Tunnel Inspection Systems market encompasses technology and services tailored for various tunnel types, including cut and cover, bored, and immersed tunnels. Cut and cover tunnels, constructed by excavating a trench and covering it, require inspections for structural integrity and leakage. Bored tunnels, created through drilling, demand assessments of ground conditions and potential wear. Immersed tunnels, built by sinking pre-fabricated sections, require evaluations for water ingress and structural sustainability. These systems enhance safety, maintenance efficiency, and lifespan management across diverse tunnel infrastructures.</p></p>
