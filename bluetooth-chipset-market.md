<p><strong>The Bluetooth Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Dual Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth chipset market is categorized primarily into two types: Single Mode and Dual Mode. Single Mode chipsets are designed exclusively for Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) applications, focusing on power efficiency and low data transmission rates, making them ideal for wearables and IoT devices. In contrast, Dual Mode chipsets support both Classic Bluetooth and BLE, enabling compatibility with a wider range of devices and facilitating seamless connections across different Bluetooth standards, beneficial for smartphones and peripherals.</p></p>
