<p><p>The industrial band saw blades market primarily features two types: bimetal and carbide tipped. Bimetal blades consist of high-speed steel teeth welded to a flexible backing, offering durability and versatility for cutting various materials. They are ideal for general-purpose applications. In contrast, carbide tipped blades have teeth made from carbide, providing superior hardness and wear resistance, making them suitable for cutting tough materials like steel and aluminum. Both types serve distinct needs in industrial cutting operations.</p></p>
