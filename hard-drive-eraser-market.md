<p><p>The hard drive eraser market is categorized into three main types: Coil Degaussers, Capacitive Discharge Degaussers, and Permanent Magnet Degaussers. Coil Degaussers generate a strong magnetic field to disrupt data stored on hard drives. Capacitive Discharge Degaussers utilize stored electrical energy to produce a magnetic pulse for data erasure. Permanent Magnet Degaussers employ powerful permanent magnets to demagnetize the media. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, portability, and effectiveness for secure data destruction.</p></p>
