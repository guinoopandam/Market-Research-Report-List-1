The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Cylindrical Mounts**: These provide linear isolation and are often used for engine and transmission applications.</p><p>2. **Bushing Mounts**: Constructed with rubber or elastomer, they absorb vibrations while allowing some movement, often found in suspension systems.</p><p>3. **Conical Mounts**: Designed for high-load applications, these mounts effectively isolate vibrations while maintaining structural integrity during dynamic conditions.</p></p>
