<p><strong>The Forged Crankshaft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integral</li><li>Assembled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forged crankshaft market can be categorized into two main types: integral and assembled crankshafts. Integral crankshafts are made from a single piece of forged metal, providing enhanced strength and durability, making them ideal for high-performance engines. In contrast, assembled crankshafts are constructed from multiple components that are individually forged and then joined together. This design allows for greater customization and flexibility in production, catering to various engine specifications while often being more cost-effective for manufacturers.</p></p>
