<p><strong>The Material Jetting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Jetting</li><li>Metal Jetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Material Jetting market comprises two primary types: Polymer Jetting and Metal Jetting. Polymer Jetting involves depositing liquid photopolymers that solidify upon UV light exposure, allowing for detailed prototypes and complex geometries in various industries. Metal Jetting, on the other hand, uses metal droplets that are precisely ejected and then fused together, enabling the production of durable metal parts with high-resolution features. Both technologies offer rapid prototyping and customization, catering to the evolving demands of modern manufacturing.</p></p>
