<p><p>The drainable ostomy care bag market consists of various types that cater to different user needs. The one-piece system integrates the pouch and skin barrier as a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, the two-piece system separates the pouch from the skin barrier, allowing for easier changing of the pouch while maintaining the barrier’s position. The skin barrier itself provides protection and a secure seal, preventing leaks and skin irritation, thus ensuring comfort and optimal care for ostomy patients.</p></p>
