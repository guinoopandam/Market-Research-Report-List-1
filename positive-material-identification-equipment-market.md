<p><ul><li>X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF)</li><li>Optical Emission Spectrometry (OES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Positive Material Identification (PMI) equipment includes techniques such as X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF) and Optical Emission Spectrometry (OES). XRF is a non-destructive method that identifies elemental compositions by measuring the fluorescent X-rays emitted from a material when exposed to radiation, offering rapid results for alloy verification. OES, on the other hand, involves exciting atoms in a sample and analyzing the light emitted, providing precise elemental analysis for metals and alloys. Both methods enhance safety and quality assurance in various industries.</p></p>
