<p><strong>The Cryoablation Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Multielectrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryoablation catheters are medical devices used to treat arrhythmias by delivering extreme cold to cardiac tissue, inducing targeted cell death. The market is segmented into two main types: single-point and multielectrode catheters. Single-point catheters focus on delivering cryogenic energy to a specific area, making them suitable for precise ablation in small regions. In contrast, multielectrode catheters have multiple points of contact, enabling broader treatment areas and more efficient ablation, allowing for quicker procedures and improved clinical outcomes.</p></p>
