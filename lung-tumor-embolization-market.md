<p><p>The lung tumor embolization market includes various techniques primarily used for treating lung tumors. Transarterial chemoembolization (TACE) combines chemotherapy with embolization to restrict tumor blood flow, enhancing drug delivery. Transarterial radioembolization (TARE) involves the injection of radioactive microspheres to target and destroy tumor cells while minimizing damage to surrounding tissue.
