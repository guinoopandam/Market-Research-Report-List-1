<p><ul><li>Rigid Laparoscope</li><li>Flexible Laparoscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laparoscopic surgery equipment market is primarily categorized into rigid and flexible laparoscopes. Rigid laparoscopes are fixed optical instruments that provide high-definition visuals and are commonly used in various surgical procedures, such as gallbladder removal and appendectomies. Flexible laparoscopy, on the other hand, utilizes flexible endoscopes that can navigate through tighter spaces within the body, making them suitable for complex surgeries. Both types contribute to minimally invasive surgery, offering benefits like reduced recovery time and less scarring for patients.</p></p>
